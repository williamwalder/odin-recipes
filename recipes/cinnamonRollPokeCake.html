Cinnamon Roll Poke Cake

Home

A delightful dessert that combines the flavors of a cinnamon roll with the simplicity of a poke cake. It starts with a soft, fluffy cake infused with cinnamon swirls, then you poke holes in it and pour a sweet, sticky glaze over the top.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups self-rising flour
  • 4 large eggs
  • ½ cup Crisco
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 1 cup milk or buttermilk, or a mix of both
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 4 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • ¼ cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour a 9- by 13-inch baking pan.
  2. With a hand-mixer or stand mixer, beat granulated sugar and Crisco until combined.
  3. Add eggs and continue to mix.
  4. Add flour, milk, and vanilla and beat for 1-2 minutes until fully combined.
  5. Spread half of the batter into the prepared pan.
  6. Combine brown sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl and mix.
  7. Sprinkle cinnamon-brown-sugar mixture over the batter in the pan, then spread the remaining cake batter on top. Swirl with a knife.
  8. Bake for 30-45 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into the cake’s center comes out clean.
  9. While the cake cools, combine milk, butter, sugar, and vanilla in a saucepan and heat until a gooey icing forms.
  10. Poke holes in the cake with a fork and pour the warm icing over top.
